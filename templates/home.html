Osteoporosis Prediction
Home Research Prediction

AIMS Math

Osteoporosis is a disease that makes bones fragile and prone to fractures. Predicting the risk of this disease is crucial for accurate diagnosis and treatment. In this study, we explore the application of Variational Inclusion Problems and Quasi-Nonexpansive Mappings to develop a risk prediction model for osteoporosis. The model utilizes medical data, such as bone density, to achieve accurate and efficient risk assessment
